🏸 Badminton Manager

The Story:
It is a sunny morning in the village of Green Valley. Everyone loves sports, but there is a problem: the village has no badminton tournament!

The Village Committee has chosen YOU to be the Manager. You have a small budget, a group of excited players, and only one week to organize everything.

If the tournament is a success, you will be a hero. If you make bad choices, the players will be sad. Can you get 30 points and win the Gold Medal?
