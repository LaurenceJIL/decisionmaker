A Day in IT at Meridian Insurance

You are Jordan Ellis, an IT Support Analyst at Meridian Insurance Group, a mid-sized company with 600 employees. Your team keeps the company's systems running — claims processing software, customer databases, internal networks, and security tools.

Today is unusually high-pressure. A server alert, a security incident, a difficult software decision, a data protection request, a vendor problem, and a major emergency will all arrive before the end of your shift. Your decisions will determine how the day ends — and whether the company stays secure and operational.

Choose carefully. Some paths are much better than others, and one wrong decision at a critical moment could end the exercise early.
