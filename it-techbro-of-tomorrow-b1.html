Glossary & Terms

  • Scale: To grow a business significantly in size and revenue.
  • Margins: The difference between the cost of making a product and its selling price.
  • Churn: The rate at which customers stop doing business with an entity.
  • Equity: Ownership of assets that may have debts or other liabilities attached to them.
  • Burn Rate: The rate at which a new company uses up its venture capital to finance overhead before generating positive cash flow.
  • ROI: Return on Investment; the ratio between net profit and cost of investment.
  • MVP: Minimum Viable Product; a version of a product with just enough features to be usable by early customers.
